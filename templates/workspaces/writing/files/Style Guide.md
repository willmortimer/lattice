# Style Guide

Decisions about voice and mechanics, so the manuscript stays consistent.

## Voice

- Point of view: (first / third limited / omniscient)
- Tense: (past / present)
- Register: (plain / lyrical / clipped)

## Mechanics

- Numbers: spell out one through nine
- Dialogue: em dash or quotes — pick one and stay with it
- Serial comma: yes

## Names and terms

| Term | Spelling | Notes |
| ---- | -------- | ----- |
| (character) | | first appears in [[Manuscript/Chapter 01]] |

## Never

- Head-hopping mid-scene
- Unattributed dialogue longer than three exchanges
