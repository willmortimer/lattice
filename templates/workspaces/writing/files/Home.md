# Writing desk

Everything for one manuscript — prose, planning, and paperwork — as ordinary files.

## Draft

- [[Manuscript/]] — chapters in reading order, starting with [[Manuscript/Chapter 01]]
- [[Style Guide]] — the rules this manuscript follows
- New chapter? Use [[Templates/Chapter]]

## Plan

- [[Outlines/]] — open `Outlines/Story Map.canvas` to arrange the structure spatially
- [[Notes/]] — quick captures land here (**⌘N**), one scene or idea per note

## Research

- [[Research/]] — background material and fact-checking, linked from the prose

## Submit

- Track pieces and venues in the **Submissions** table (`Submissions.data`)
- Move dead drafts to [[Archive/]] instead of deleting them
