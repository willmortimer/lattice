## How this workspace works

This folder uses a **PARA**-inspired layout: capture in [[Inbox/]], push time-bound work into [[Projects/]], maintain [[Areas/]], and file reference material in [[Library/]]. Day-to-day notes live in [[Journal/]]; finished material moves to [[Archive/]]. Track lightweight tasks in the seeded **Tasks** table.
