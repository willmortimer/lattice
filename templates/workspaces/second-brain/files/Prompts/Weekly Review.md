# Weekly Review

Run this against [[MOCs/Knowledge Base]] and [[Inbox/]]:

1. **Empty Inbox** — triage per [[Notes/Capture Workflow]].
2. **Refresh evergreens** — pick two notes from [[Notes/Evergreen Notes]] and improve titles or links.
3. **Schedule repetition** — choose notes for [[Notes/Spaced Repetition]].
4. **Update the map** — add new entries to [[MOCs/Knowledge Base]] for mature clusters.

Summarize what changed and what to read next.
