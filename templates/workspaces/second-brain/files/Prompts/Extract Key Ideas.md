# Extract Key Ideas

Given material in [[Sources/]] (or pasted text):

1. Extract **atomic claims** suitable for [[Notes/]].
2. Apply [[Notes/Progressive Summarization]] — bold, then restate in your words.
3. Link each claim to related notes, especially [[Notes/Zettelkasten Primer]] and [[Notes/Linking Philosophy]].
4. Suggest one candidate [[Notes/Evergreen Notes|evergreen note]] title.

Output proposed note titles and wiki links only — no folder moves.
