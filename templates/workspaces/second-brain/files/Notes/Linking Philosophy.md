# Linking Philosophy

Folders sort; links *relate*. A second brain earns its name when [[Notes/]] form a graph you can traverse in multiple directions.

## Practices

- Link **up** to maps like [[MOCs/Knowledge Base]].
- Link **sideways** between peers — e.g. [[Notes/Capture Workflow]] ↔ [[Notes/Progressive Summarization]].
- Link **down** to evidence in [[Sources/]].

## Why it matters

Backlinks surface surprise adjacency. That is the compounding return on [[Notes/Zettelkasten Primer|atomic notes]].

Use [[Prompts/Synthesize Connections]] when the graph feels sparse.
