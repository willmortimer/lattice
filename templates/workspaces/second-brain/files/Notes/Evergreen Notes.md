# Evergreen Notes

Evergreen notes stay useful years later. They are concise, densely linked, and revised in place rather than duplicated.

## Traits

- **Atomic** — one idea; see [[Notes/Zettelkasten Primer]].
- **Concept-oriented** — titles name ideas, not meetings or dates.
- **Heavily linked** — neighbors include [[Notes/Linking Philosophy]] and [[Notes/Spaced Repetition]].

## Maintenance

Revisit notes from [[MOCs/Knowledge Base]] during [[Prompts/Weekly Review]]. When a note grows, split it and link siblings.

Distillation path: [[Notes/Progressive Summarization]] → evergreen → [[Notes/Spaced Repetition]].
