# Progressive Summarization

Layer highlights on source material so future-you can skim depth on demand.

## Layers

1. Capture excerpts into [[Sources/]] or [[Inbox/]].
2. **Bold** the best passages.
3. Summarize in your own words in [[Notes/]].
4. Promote the insight to an [[Notes/Evergreen Notes|evergreen note]].

Pairs with [[Notes/Capture Workflow]] for intake and [[Notes/Spaced Repetition]] for retention.

Orient from [[MOCs/Knowledge Base]] when choosing what to distill next.
