# Capture Workflow

Capture is a separate mode from thinking. The goal is zero friction: park the thought in [[Inbox/]] and return to what you were doing.

## Principles

1. **Separate capture from organization.** Triage later from [[MOCs/Knowledge Base]].
2. **Prefer atomic notes.** One claim per page — see [[Notes/Zettelkasten Primer]].
3. **Link early.** Even a rough link to [[Notes/Linking Philosophy]] beats an orphan note.

## Triage checklist

- Fleeting → leave in [[Inbox/]] or delete after processing.
- Reference → move summary to [[Notes/]] and source to [[Sources/]].
- Durable insight → rewrite as an [[Notes/Evergreen Notes|evergreen note]].
