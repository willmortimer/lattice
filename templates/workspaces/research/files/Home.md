# Research home

Start with [[Questions]] — then follow the pipeline below.

1. Capture unprocessed material in [[Inbox/]].
2. Keep original references in [[Sources/]] — see [[Sources/Example Paper]], built from [[Templates/Literature Note]].
3. Develop your thinking in [[Notes/]].
4. Log runs and outcomes in **Experiments log** (seeded table).
5. Keep reproducible work in [[Experiments/]] — protocol pages start from [[Templates/Experiment]].
6. Publish durable results from [[Outputs/]].
