# Market Notes

Working assumptions for positioning — link to [[Research/Competitor Analysis]] and
[[Research/Interview Synthesis]].

## Segments

| Segment | Pain | Lattice fit |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Individual knowledge workers | Tool sprawl | Unified files + search |
| Small product teams | Roadmap + research scattered | Pages + canvas + tables |
| Data-curious builders | SQL without ops burden | `.data` apps beside Markdown |

## Pricing hypotheses

- Free local core; paid sync/collab later (out of scope for this template)
- Teams value export and auditability over infinite block types

## Open questions

- How much calendar view usage appears in CRM-style tables?
- Do users expect `Templates/` to mirror daily-note plugins from other tools?
