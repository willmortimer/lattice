# Brief

## Outcome

What will exist when this project is complete?

Describe the deliverable in one or two sentences — something a stakeholder could recognize as done.

## Context

Why does this matter now?

- What problem or opportunity triggered this work?
- Who benefits when it ships?

## Constraints

- **Time** — target date or sprint window
- **Scope** — what is explicitly out of scope
- **Dependencies** — teams, systems, or approvals you need

## Success signals

How will you know the project succeeded?
