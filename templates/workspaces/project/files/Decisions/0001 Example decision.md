## Context

What situation or question prompted this decision?

## Decision

We will use lightweight ADRs in [[Decisions/]] and the [[Templates/Decision]] pattern for new records.

## Consequences

- **Positive** — decisions stay traceable alongside [[Plan]] and [[Brief]].
- **Negative** — requires discipline to write them before momentum carries the team forward.

## Alternatives considered

- Ad-hoc notes in [[Meetings/]] only — harder to find later.
- External wiki — splits context away from project files.
